# DukanMitra — AI Employees for SMEs
Product Requirements Document · v1.0 · Hackathon Edition
One-line pitch: "DukanMitra provides AI employees for SMEs that autonomously manage sales, payments, and inventory through WhatsApp."

## Core Product Goals
- Reduce manual operational workload for SMEs
- Improve customer response speed (target: < 3s)
- Increase payment recovery rate
- Prevent inventory stockouts
- 24/7 autonomous business assistance

## AI Agents
- **AI Sales Employee**: Customer NLU, product recommendations, quotes, orders, payment links.
- **AI Recovery Employee**: Payment monitoring, tiered reminders (friendly → firm → urgent), owner notifications.
- **AI Inventory Employee**: Stock tracking, demand prediction, reorder recommendations.

## Dashboard Modules
- **Overview**: KPIs (Sales, Pending Invoices, Stock Alerts), AI actions, live conversations.
- **Sales**: Orders, quotes, conversion rates, customer logs.
- **Recovery**: Overdue buckets, reminder history, recovery rates.
- **Inventory**: Stock levels, demand forecasting, reorder suggestions.
